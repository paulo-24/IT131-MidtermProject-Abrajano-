Buluan Island: a Beach Destination in Zamboanga Sibugay

January 22, 2022. Me and my family visited this Island and luckily, Me and my Family are the only people to be there at that time because we go there early in the morning. White sand beaches and crystal-clear waters may be found on Buluan Island. Buluan Island can provide solitude and peace, even though the uncombed sand isn't exceptionally fine and powdery. Although the island is privately owned, no visitors are charged an admission fee. The island is not yet developed commercially. Visitors should buy food and beverages from the town because there are neither resorts nor restaurants on the island. Visitors can use the bamboo tables and bungalows without paying anything. At the capital town of Ipil, one need not to go far just to have a taste of what Zamboanga Sibugay has to offer. Located some 7 kilometers from the port of Ipil, Buluan Island is a famous beach destination among Sibugaynons and is now slowly gaining popularity in nearby towns and provinces.
